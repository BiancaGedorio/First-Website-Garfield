About us!


Welcome Parents! This website is a fan site of a cartoon character called Garfield from The Garfield Show tv series.
It is a CGI animated series, produced by Jim Davis. The show aired from November 2009 until October 2016.
The main characters of this show are Garfield, Odie, Jon Arbuckle, Nermal, Arlene and Dr. Liz Wilson.
